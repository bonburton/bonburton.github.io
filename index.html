What is the school snackshop?

The Lanna International School Snackshop is a snackshop located within the cafeteria at Lanna International School Thailand. It is ran by the year 10 secondary students.

The snackshop is the main food/beverage shops at Lanna International School Thailand. It is ran by a manager elected every month, and is hygenic since it is cleaned regularly.

Snackshop events

Sadly, the Snackshop has only participated in two events last year; "The Witches" and a Halloween event. Hopefully we will participate more in the following years.
